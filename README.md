Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: March 20, 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains how Party Animals Selector ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses information when you use our mobile application ("App").

The App is a simple single-screen application that displays banner advertisements provided by Google AdMob. We do not collect any personal information directly from you.

### 1\. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or process any personal data such as name, email, phone number, or location from users.

Third-party service providers (specifically Google AdMob) may collect certain information automatically when ads are displayed in the App. This may include:

*   Device information (e.g., device type, operating system, unique device identifiers like Advertising ID)
*   IP address
*   Ad interaction data (e.g., views, clicks)
*   Approximate location derived from IP

This data is collected for purposes such as serving ads, measuring ad performance, preventing fraud, and (where permitted) showing personalized/interest-based ads.

### 2\. Third-Party Services – Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display banner advertisements. Google may use cookies, web beacons, or similar technologies to collect information as a result of ad serving.

For more information about how Google collects and uses data for advertising, please review:

*   [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
*   [AdMob and Interest-Based Ads](https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543)

You can opt out of personalized (interest-based) advertising by visiting your device settings or Google's Ads Settings: [https://adssettings.google.com](https://adssettings.google.com).

### 3\. Consent and Compliance

The App uses Google's User Messaging Platform to request consent where required (e.g., for users in the EEA, UK, or other regulated regions under GDPR). We respect your choices regarding data collection for advertising.

On iOS devices, we comply with Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework.

### 4\. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or 16 in some jurisdictions). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
